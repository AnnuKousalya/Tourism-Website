TOURISM IN RAJASTHAN

FEATURED PLACES

Jal Mahal

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Jal Mahal (meaning "Water Palace") is a palace in the middle of the Man Sagar Lake in Jaipur city, the capital of the state of Rajasthan, India.

Jaipur,Rajasthan


Central Park

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

There are a few other attractions around this Park like the Musical fountain, a temple and stone statues.The tower at the central park is huge and has the tallest tricolour - National Flag of our country.

Rajasthan


Sambar Lake

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Sambhar is known for the Sambhar Salt Lake, which is the largest inland salt lake in India. It derives its name from Shakambhari Devi, Goddess of the Chauhan clan, whose 2500 year old temple is located 28 km from the town.

Sambhar, Rajasthan


Sariska National Park

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Sariska Tiger Reserve is well nestled in the Aravali Hills covering 800 sq km area divided into the grasslands, dry deciduous forests, sheer cliffs and rocky landscape.

Rajasthan


Hawa Mahal

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

The Hawa Mahal is a palace in the city of Jaipur, India. Built from red and pink sandstone, it is on the edge of the City Palace, Jaipur, and extends to the Zenana, or women's chambers.

Jaipur,Rajasthan

1000 pillar temple

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

The Thousand Pillar Temple or Rudreswara Swamy Temple [వేయి స్తంభాల గుడి]is a historic Hindu temple located in the town of Hanamakonda, Telangana State, India. It is dedicated to Lord Shiva, Vishnu and Surya.

Kunthala Waterfalls

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

It is the highest waterfall in the state with a height of 150 feet (45 meters). These waterfalls are in the dense forests inhabited by the Gonds. Kunta in Gondi and Telugu language means pond.

Adilabad,Telangana


Ranganathaswamy Temple

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Sri Ranganathaswamy temple is a 400 year old Vaishnavite temple dedicated to Lord Ranganatha, a form of the Hindu god Vishnu, which is located at Jiyaguda in Hyderabad, Telangana,India.

Jiyaguda, Telangana

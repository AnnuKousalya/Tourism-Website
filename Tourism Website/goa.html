TOURISM IN GOA

FEATURED PLACES

Baga Beach

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Counted amongst the most popular and lively beaches in Goa, Baga(Beach) is literally an extension of Calangute.

Baga, Goa


Sahyadri mountains

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

The Sahyadri mountains fall to the East of South Goa and this makes it home to Goa’s best forest covers.

Goa


Soro–The Village Pub

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Soro is known to have a lot of partying and a little bit of everything. At Soro, you can mix with a young and energetic group while playing pool, enjoying live music, or trying food from their live grill.

Soro,Goa


LPK Waterfront – Love, Passion, and Karma

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

When you think of a traditional nightclub, you probably imagine one with DJs playing all night, people drinking alcohol, and a love-filled atmosphere pervading everything. LPK Waterfront is just that.

Nerul River, Goa


Reis Magos Fort

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Reis Magos Fort, which dates to 1493, was formerly a fortification built as a line of defense. The fort is now a popular destination in Goa for travelers wishing to escape the beaches and explore the state’s interesting history.

Velha,Goa

Victoria Memorial Hall

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

The Victoria Memorial is a large marble building on the Maidan in Central Kolkata, having its entrance on the Queen's Way. It is the largest monument to a monarch anywhere in the world, it stands in 64 acres of gardens.

Kolkata,West Bengal


Howrah Bridge

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

The Howrah Bridge is a balanced cantilever bridge over the Hooghly River in West Bengal,Commissioned in 1943

Tiger Hill

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Tiger Hill is eminently known for its splendid sights of the early morning sunrise where you can witness a panoramic view of Mount Everest and Mount Kanchenjunga together.

Darjeeling,West Bengal


Taraknath Temple

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Taraknath Temple is a famous Hindu shrine located in the town of Tarakeswar in Hooghly district of West Bengal, India. The temple enshrines a Shayambhu linga of Lord Shiva. The temple is believed to be constructed in 1729 AD by Raja Bharamalla Rao.

Tarakeswar,West Bengal
